Our Goal

Have you felt lonely before? Tired of typical social night-outs and small-talk that gets you nowhere? Well, you’re not alone, with 1 in 3 Australians reporting feeling socially disconnected.

Our goal is to solve the loneliness crisis and provide an intentional space for adults to form genuine relationships through genuine conversations that last beyond the first "hello". We've heard the cries of those who want something deeper and far more fulfilling than a regular night-out. So we pair thought-provoking talks with shared drinks and meals, as we believe that's the best way to form meaningful relationships — not over screens, but over tables.

The result? A night of good food and drink that stimulates the mind, builds genuine social relationships and provides curious people with something far more fulfilling than just a regular night out.

Join us to redefine how social and intellectual experiences should be amid an increasingly disconnected world. One talk and one bite at a time!
